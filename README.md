## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Amazon Global Sales Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, market distribution, customer profitability, and product insights across different regions.

The dashboard provides meaningful business insights through data visualization and KPI tracking to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives
The main objectives of this project are:

- Analyze global sales performance
- Track KPIs and return metrics
- Identify top and low-performing products
- Understand market-wise sales distribution
- Visualize sales by region
- Analyze customer profitability trends

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI
- Excel Dataset
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Dashboard Design
- Business Analytics

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. KPI Cards
The dashboard includes key business metrics:

- **Sales Projection:** 3.41M
- **Product Units Sold:** 3.242K
- **KPI Value:** 48K
- **Returns:** 372

These KPIs help in quickly understanding overall business performance.

---

### 2. Year-wise Sales Filter
Interactive year filters (2012–2015) allow users to analyze sales trends across different years.

---

### 3. Sales by Segment
Displays sales contribution across customer segments:

- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

This helps identify which segment generates maximum revenue.

---

### 4. Sales by Market
Market-wise analysis across:

- Asia Pacific
- Europe
- USCA
- LATAM
- Africa

This visualization helps compare global market performance.

---

### 5. Sales by Region (Map Visualization)
Interactive map showing regional sales distribution globally.

This helps understand:

- High-performing regions
- Market penetration
- Geographic business opportunities

---

### 6. Profit by Customer Name
Identifies top profit-generating customers.

Useful for:
- Customer segmentation
- High-value customer identification

---

### 7. Top 5 Profit by Product
Highlights products generating the highest profits.

Helps in:
- Product strategy
- Inventory prioritization
- Business growth planning

---

### 8. Bottom 5 Profit by Product
Shows underperforming products with low or negative profit.

Useful for:
- Cost reduction
- Product optimization
- Business improvement

---

## Business Insights
Some important findings from the dashboard:

- Consumer segment contributes the highest sales.
- Certain markets perform significantly better than others.
- Top-performing products contribute major profits.
- Some products generate negative profits and may require optimization.
- Sales distribution varies significantly by geography.
